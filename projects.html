What We Do

Our Projects

Every project is a promise kept to the Anitanguri community.

Since our founding in 2022, Anitanguri Youth Association has completed 3 community projects spanning education, health, and infrastructure. Each one was planned, funded, and executed by our youth — for our people.

Community
2023

Community Parliament

A dedicated space built for the people of Anitanguri to gather, converse, and connect — any time of day.

Recognizing the lack of a communal space, A.Y.A constructed a base parliament where community members can sit, hold discussions, and spend their leisure time together. The space has quickly become a hub for community life — including women's SUSU group meetings and community gatherings.

Education
2025

School Supplies Donation

Putting the tools of learning directly into the hands of students — from KG all the way to JHS.

In 2025, A.Y.A organized a school supplies donation drive that reached over 20 students across kindergarten, primary, and junior high school levels. Each student received a package including uniforms, exercise books, mathematical sets, pens, pencils, and erasers — ensuring no child is held back by a lack of basic materials.

Health
2025

Health Insurance Renewal

Ensuring our most vulnerable community members have access to healthcare when they need it most.

In 2025, A.Y.A took steps to improve healthcare access by renewing the health insurance cards of children and elders in the community. This initiative ensures that those who are most vulnerable are not left without medical cover, reflecting our commitment to the health and wellbeing of every member of Anitanguri.
