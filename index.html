Who We Are

We Couldn't Wait — So We Acted

Anitanguri Youth Association was born from a simple truth: our community could not wait. Recognizing urgent needs across education, health, and community development — and knowing government intervention could take decades — the youth of Anitanguri took matters into their own hands.

Together, we have built a base parliament, supported students in local schools, and continue to grow our impact one project at a time.
