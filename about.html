Our Story

We Couldn't Wait — So We Acted

In January 2022, a group of young friends from Anitanguri gathered with a shared concern: their community was falling behind, and no one was coming to help. Recognizing urgent needs across education, health, and community development — and knowing government intervention could take decades — they decided to take matters into their own hands.

What started as a conversation among friends quickly grew into something much bigger. The Anitanguri Youth Association was officially formed, uniting the youth of the community under one purpose: to serve, to build, and to uplift those around them.

Since then, A.Y.A has distributed school supplies to students, renewed health insurance cards for children, and built a community parliament space — and this is only the beginning.

🔭

Our Vision

A thriving Anitanguri community where every young person has access to education, healthcare, and equal opportunities to grow and succeed.

🎯

Our Mission

To empower the Anitanguri community by initiating and executing youth-led projects in education, health, and community development — building a better future without waiting for others to act.

Our Objectives

Clear goals that guide our work and measure our progress.

01

Education Support

To provide educational materials and supplies to students in Anitanguri and surrounding communities, ensuring no child is left behind due to lack of resources.

02

Community Infrastructure

A.Y.A Milestones

A look at how far we have come since January 2022.

A.Y.A is Founded

A group of young friends from Anitanguri come together and officially form the Anitanguri Youth Association.

2022

Community Parliament Built

A.Y.A constructs a base parliament — a dedicated space for community members to gather, converse, and connect.
